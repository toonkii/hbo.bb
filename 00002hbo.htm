Bus Routes

Transport Board Bus Routes 11A (to Eddy's Village) or 16 (to St. Patrick's) - get off at CARTERS and cross Wildey Main Road from gas station to Observatory Road; follow road to next junction and turn right.

ZR Van Route 8 (Forde's Road) - get off at Observatory Road and walk up incline approx. 100 ft to the Observatory (domed building on left hand at top of the incline).
