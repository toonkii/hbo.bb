Membership

The Barbados Astronomical Society encourages and welcomes new members, and membership is open to all those who are interested in astronomy and wish to learn more about it.

Members of the Society are entitled to free entry to the Harry Bayley Observatory on Friday evenings, as well as to the Society's special events. The annual subscription fees are as follows:

Bds$
100.00 - Full member
  50.00 - Associate member
  30.00 - Student member
