Diplomatic Immunity (2006) - by J. Berkey

John Berkey

Born: August 13, 1932, North Dakota, USA
Died: April 29, 2008

Perhaps the most widely respected of all space artists and one of the giants in the history of science fiction art, John Berkey had a long and distinguished career. He started as a professional artist in the 1950s, working for various studios before spending eight formative years at Brown & Bigelow, the major calendar-publishing company of the day; there he developed his amazing landscape skills in depicting scenes of Americana and the like. On turning freelance he soon brought his skills and dazzling neo-impressionist style to the field of science fiction with great success.
