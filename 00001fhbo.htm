Layers, probably sedimentary in origin, have undergone erosion in this image from NASA's Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter (MRO) of Shalbatana Valles, a prominent channel that cuts through Xanthe Terra.

The erosion has produced several small mesas and exposed light-toned material that may differ in composition from the surrounding material.
