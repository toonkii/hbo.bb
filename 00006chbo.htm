1988

25th Anniversary of Harry Bayley Observatory

The 25th anniversary of Harry Bayley Observatory was celebrated, in part, by the issue of a First Day Cover of four commemorative Barbados postage stamps on November 28. The denominations were 25¢, 65¢, 75¢ and $2; depicting respectively Dr. Harry Bayley and the Observatory; the range of night sky that can be seen from Barbados; the Andromeda galaxy & the Observatory's Celestron telescope; and finally, the Orion constellation.
