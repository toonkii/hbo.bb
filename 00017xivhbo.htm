In the constellation Canis Major, Thor's Helmet is an emission nebula some 15,000 lightyears distant, and about 30 lightyears in size. At the centre of the nebula's bubble is a Wolf-Rayet star, an extremely hot giant star whose intense stellar wind is causing the nebula's gases to billow. The nebula's catalogue reference is NGC 2359.
