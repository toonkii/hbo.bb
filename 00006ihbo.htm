Part of the Barbados Astronomical Society's exhibit at the 2010 SciTech Expo was the static display of the Society's portable, 8-inch Celestron telescope. This telescope was recently acquired through a generous donation of funds from the United States Embassy in Barbados. This equipment will enable the Society to be mobile, and the B.A.S is grateful to the U.S. Embassy for its kind assistance in making this purchase possible.
