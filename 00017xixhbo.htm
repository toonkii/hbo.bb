Within the Milky Way and approximately 7,000 to 8,000 light-years away from Earth, in the Northern Hemisphere constellation Cepheus, the Wizard Nebula [NGC 7380] is a star nursery, itself about 100 light-years in diameter. Within it lies an open cluster of young stars, of around 5 million years old.
